01 · The reason

Why another timer app?

Most productivity tools are loud. Notifications, streaks, percentages, gamification — a software industrial complex of nudges. Lumen leans the other way: one session, one screen, one journal entry. Done.

I designed it for myself first. After a year of using it daily I realised other designers wanted the same thing, so I made it real.

02 · The approach

Design what to leave out.

Every screen had a budget — three meaningful elements, max. If a fourth wanted in, something else had to leave. Most decisions were subtractive.

"The hardest part of designing Lumen was deciding what not to ship."

The whole motion system runs on a single spring, retimed. No bouncy animations, no easter-egg confetti. The most exciting thing in the app is a 600ms cross-fade.

03 · The outcome

It found its people.

Lumen launched in October 2024 and crossed 10k downloads in the first week, mostly through word-of-mouth in design communities. It's been featured by Sidebar, Mobbin and Designspells, and was Product Hunt's #2 product of the day. Most importantly: people keep using it after week 4.
