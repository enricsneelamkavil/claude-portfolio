How I got here.

  1. 2026

    Sr. Product Designer · Method Current

    Leading design for a Series B B2B SaaS platform. Owning the design system, AI features, and onboarding for ~12k teams.

  2. 2024

    Founder & Designer · Lumen Headline

    Built and shipped Lumen — a focus-timer + journaling app — solo. iOS + macOS. ~38k downloads. 14 months of nights and weekends.

  3. 2023

    Founding Designer · Sona

    Voice journaling app, seed-stage. Set up the design language, end-to-end product, motion vocabulary and brand.

  4. 2022

    Product Designer · Fennec Bank

    Reimagined the new-customer onboarding flow. 19% drop in abandonment, shipped to 4 markets in 8 months.

  5. 2022

    B.Des · NID Ahmedabad

    Bachelor's in Industrial Design. Graduated with distinction. Final-year project on tactile interfaces for low-vision users.
